Instructions 

1. Enter your name in the Name box.
 
2. Press the Roll button to get a number and the next person presses it to see who goes first.

3. When you know who goes first then that person presses the roll button and a question will popup.

4. If you answer incorrect you will not move.
   
5. You will then keep going around the board and you will win if you reach the end first.
